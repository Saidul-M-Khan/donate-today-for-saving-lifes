About Us

Following a severe flood in early June that affected around 4 million people, the same area was struck by record-breaking rains beginning on June 15. The majority of Sylhet has been submerged once more, with water levels continuing to rise, aggravating the situation of people still living in temporary shelters and exposing them to harsh weather and waterborne infections.




However, further assistance from philanthropic organizations and development partners is urgently required. As the days pass, the demand for rescue and aid grows in flood-affected communities.
